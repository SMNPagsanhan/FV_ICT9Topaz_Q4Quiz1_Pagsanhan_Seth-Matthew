The Philippines

Palawan

  • Situated in the Mimaropa region, Palawan, formally known as the Province of Palawan, is an archipelagic province of the Philippines.
  • Palawan is a nature lover's paradise, offering everything from immaculate beaches and glistening oceans to lush forests and breathtaking limestone cliffs.

Bicol

  • Bicol is a region in the Philippines that includes neighboring island provinces and the southern portion of Luzon Island.
  • With its historical landmarks, eco-tourism, and water sports, Bicol is a location that is perfect for everyone. In addition, there are several breathtaking waterfalls, Bulusan Volcano Natural Park, and the well-known Mayon Volcano. Not to be overlooked are the manta rays and whale sharks, which have made Bicol famous.

Siargao

  • Siargao is an island in the Philippine Sea that resembles a teardrop and is located 196 kilometers southeast of Tacloban.
  • Siargao, dubbed the "Surfing Capital of the Philippines," is primarily to blame for the spread of surfing throughout the nation.
